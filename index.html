About Al Fuhaid Trading

40 Years of Excellence in Industrial Supply

Established in 1983 (1403 هـ), Al Fuhaid Trading has been a trusted name in the industrial sector, providing high-quality mechanical parts and components to businesses across Saudi Arabia.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for numerous industrial enterprises throughout the region.

Based in Jeddah, we combine traditional business values with modern supply chain solutions to meet the evolving needs of the industrial sector.
