About Al Fuhaid Trading

40 Years of Excellence in Industrial Supply

Established in 1983 (1403 هـ), Al Fuhaid Trading has been a trusted name in the industrial sector, providing high-quality mechanical parts and components to businesses across Saudi Arabia.

Our commitment to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for numerous industrial enterprises throughout the region.

Based in Jeddah, we combine traditional business values with modern supply chain solutions to meet the evolving needs of the industrial sector.

Our Products

Couplings & Spiders

Flexible & rigid couplings, sleeves, and spiders from top brands such as TB Woods, Lovejoy, Rexnord, KTR, Fenner, and more.

Mechanical Seals

Original and replacement seals (John Crane, Burgmann, AESSEAL, Pacseal, Chesterton, Flygt, Grundfos, KSB, Lowara, DP) with fabrication and repair services.

Gaskets & Packing

Spiral wound gaskets, flange gaskets, and gasket sheets made from non-asbestos, graphite, metallic, rubber, and cork materials. Gland packing available.

Bearings, Chains & Power Transmission

Bearings, sprockets, chains, belts, and oil seals for industrial power transmission systems.

Stainless Steel & Teflon Products

SS wire mesh, perforated sheets, Teflon sheets, and round bars (FIFE, PE, HDPE, PU) available in different sizes and diameters.

Industrial & Safety Supplies

PVC curtains, packing rolls, buffers, mountings, and general industrial & safety materials for workshop and factory use.
